Bio

Abhijin Adiga

 

Welcome to my home page!

I am a research associate professor at the Network Systems Science and Advanced Computing (NSSAC) division, Biocomplexity Institute and Initiative, University of Virginia. Broadly, my research areas are combinatorics, design and analysis of algorithms, computational modeling, algorithmic game theory, and machine learning. For the past few years, my main focus has been the study of propagation processes over networks, also referred to as network dynamics. My interests lie in the theoretical foundations of these processes as well as applying them to study complex phenomena such as invasive species spread, infectious diseases and other socio-technical systems.
